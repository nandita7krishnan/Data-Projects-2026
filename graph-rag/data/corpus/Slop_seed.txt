# Slop

Category: Concept | Difficulty: beginner

Source: Seed knowledge base


## Definition

A colloquial term for low-quality, high-volume AI-generated content that lacks originality, accuracy, or genuine value — produced by AI models used indiscriminately without human curation. A growing concern in search quality, social media, and publishing.

## Example Use Cases

- Identifying AI content farms
- Content quality filtering
- Understanding LLM misuse
- AI literacy discussions
