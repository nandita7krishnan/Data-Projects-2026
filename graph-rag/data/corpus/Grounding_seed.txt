# Grounding

Category: Technique | Difficulty: intermediate

Source: Seed knowledge base


## Definition

Connecting LLM outputs to verifiable, real-world information sources. RAG is the primary technique for grounding — the LLM's answer is anchored to retrieved documents, reducing hallucination.

## Example Use Cases

- Factual QA systems
- Legal and medical AI
- Enterprise knowledge management
