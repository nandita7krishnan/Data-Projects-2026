# AI Guardrails

Category: Concept | Difficulty: intermediate

Source: Seed knowledge base


## Definition

Safety mechanisms — technical, policy, or procedural — designed to constrain AI system behavior within ethical, legal, and operational boundaries. Guardrails prevent harmful outputs, enforce content policies, and ensure compliance.

## Example Use Cases

- Preventing toxic content generation
- Blocking PII leakage
- Enforcing brand voice
- Regulatory compliance for AI products

## Related Tools

Guardrails AI, NeMo Guardrails, LlamaGuard, Azure AI Content Safety
